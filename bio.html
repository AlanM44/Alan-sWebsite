Biography

Hello! I am Alan Moody, a passionate engineering student and outdoor enthusiast. My journey in engineering has been fueled by a love for problem-solving and innovation. I spent two years studying Mechanical engineering at Oregon State University, where I gained a strong foundation in engineering principles and practices. I have since transferred to Oxford Brookes University to study Motorsport engineering. When I'm not working on exciting projects, you can find me on a mountain bike trail, weightlifting or contributing to Oxford Brookes Racing.
